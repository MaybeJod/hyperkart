General Info

🗓️ Date: Friday, October 11th

🕒 Time: 16:00

📍 Location: Hyper Island Stockholm campus

How to Participate:

Only Hyper students and Alumni can compete in the tournament.

Sign up to race! Spots are limited, so make sure you register ASAP to secure your place on the track.

You can invite friends to join as racers or just come and watch the action!

General Rules:

Races will feature 4 players at a time.

Each player can choose any character and vehicle.

You also get to choose one racecourse.

After each race, 2 players will advance to the next stage until we crown our champion!

⚠️ Spots are limited - First come, first serve 🏁✨
